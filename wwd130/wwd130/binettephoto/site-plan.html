Content

Home page

People are going to come to my website because they are looking to see what I can do with my camera. They are looking for information on booking, prices, the work that ive done, and what I offer for services. Once they get into the site they need to see some of my work front and center at a decent resolution to where they can see the quality but not too much that it makes the site slow. The idea is to have evenly spaced buttons with the pictures inside of them to take them to the respective subpage that showes the work in more detail along with a small effect that shows that you are interacting with the page. On the top of the page my logo is going to be top center as the home button while being flanked by “work” and “contact” following the color scheme. There will be a minimallistic theme that accentuates the photos in the showcase. This includes a flat grey backround and simmilar layout to other photography websites. The information that ill need in my website is going to be large, this includes all the pictures that I think are important as well as an area for references and a bio section. All of this needs to be put in an acessable way that lets people see what they need to first while not having any non important information the website also needs to look moddern and clean as to not distract from the pictures that it is trying to showcase. There needs to be information on where I am located, what services I provide, and how much some of these cost on the spot as well as different price options.

Work showcase

On the work page there will be subpages that contain the appropreate work in a compressed form to make it load faster as well as a larger image to see in fullscreen mode there will be three to start with but the layout will be made in a way that it can be expanded later on and keep the form it is in now.

Contact Page

The other option in the home page will be a page to contact. In this page there will be a picture of me aswell as a banner image to add a splash of color. Inside of this there is going to be my name and a small bio something along the lines of “I surve the dc metro area and will travel to seal the memories of your event forever. Call or email to schedule a consult.”there will also be a rough pricing guide at the bottom of the page allowing people to get an idea what they are getting into. Through the website the visitors will want to find a way to contact me and what the process of some of these activities are going to be. The information thats there needs to be presented in a professional way as to make the potential customer want to get in contact with me to schedule a call or meeting to discuss what the best option for them is going to be.
